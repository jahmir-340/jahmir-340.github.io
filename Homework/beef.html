Grilled Rib Eye Steak with Italian Salsa Verde

    Things you will need

      2 lbs rib eye steak
      2 tbsp butter
      Salt and pepper to taste
      12 oz tub Red salsa
      1.5 pounds of boneless chicken breast
      1/4 cup of homemade taco seasoning or 1 store brought

    Method of cooking

  1. Add all ingredients to a Crock-Pot.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or high for 2 hours.
  3. Shred the chicken and put back into the juices.
  4. Add all ingredients to the pressure cooker and mix up as best as you can. Seal the lid and set to pressure cook high for 10 minutes. Allow vent to slow release for 20 minutes. Once meat is finished cooking, shred the chicken and put it back into the juices.
  5. Then when its taken out you can serve and enjoy
