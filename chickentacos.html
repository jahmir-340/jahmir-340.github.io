Toasty's Great Chicken tacos

    Things you will need

  1. Taco shell
  2. Chicken Breast
  3. Goya Taco seasoning
  4. Salt
  5. Flour
  6. Pepper
  7. Red crushed pepper flakes
  8. Oil
  9. Different toppings of your choice

    10. Method of cooking

  10. Put taco shells on a tray and put it on the over on high heat so that they crisp up
  11. While the shells crisp up cut up the chicken breast into finely sliced strips
  12. Put into a big bowl all the spices and seasoning
  13. Put into another bowl just flour
  14. Put oil in a pan on mediuem heat
  15. Dunk the strips in the flour
  16. Then dunk them into the seasoing bowl
  17. Coating it all around
  18. Then put them into the hot oil til golden brown
  19. Cut them up and top with your fav toppings
  20. Lastly enjoy
